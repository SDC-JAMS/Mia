How is Airbnb affecting your borough?

Data Analysis using Inside Airbnb, to investigate neighbourhood characteristics.

Context

About Airbnb

Established in 2008, Airbnb has become iconic of the sharing economy, as a peer-to-peer online platform linking up hosts renting out their residential properties with guests seeking short-term apartment. Having experienced dramatic growth since its inception, the platform now features over 1.5 million properties and over 34,000 cities including London.

Popularity

Neighbourhood Popularity and Reputation

Location data of London's Airbnb apartments exhibit clustering tendencies within the city centre. This leads us to investigate the impact of the neighbourhoods themselves on ratings or average prices of Airbnb listings within these clusters. By investigating neighbourhood factors influencing both Airbnb reputation scores and prices, we potentially provide insight into how local neighbourhoods affect host performance and how location choice may allow hosts to capitalise on their reputation.

Review Sentiments

Spatial Polarisation

User reviews crucially influence the success and popularity of businesses, and should be accounted for when examining factors affecting the performance of sharing accommodation economies such as Airbnbs. Here we focus on the influence of guest reviews on defining neighbourhood characteristics and comparing them with the host advertised overview in their listings. This assists with forming a better understanding of the influential factors in regards to accommodation experiences.
