Home Aims

Can we predict the characteristics of your neighbourhood of interest to enhance your rental experience?

Objectives

Ever wondered how prices, ratings and reviews relate to spatial attributes of the listings neighbourhood?

Us too! Our aim is to find out if listings with higher ratings, prices and reviews correlate positively with the location or socio-economic status of a neighbourhood. We seek to understand if Airbnb experience could provide insights into the functionality of that neighbourhood. We achieve these objectives by examining four aspects, depending on your role as a user:

You're a Guest

You would like to know what makes the attraction, reputation and functionality of the neighbourhood you're staying in. You would also like to see the impact of your reviews on determining those characteristics and know where your opinion stands compared to reviews in other areas.

You're a Host

You would like to know how the different neighbourhoods cluster according to their ratings, and where your listing fits within this. You would also like to know the factors influencing the overall listing rating & the average price per night according to your location.

You're a Neighbour

You would like to explore characteristics of your neighbourhood you did not know of, and get the chance to rediscover your community. And would you like to learn about rentals in the age of digitalisation?

You're a potential guest

You would like to know in advance the friendliness of the neighbourhood you're staying in by discovering how review sentiments, word patterns and trends reveal spatial polarisation.
